Chicken Alfredo

Perfect Chicken Alfredo

A classic dish enjoyed by many, and can be prepared with little time or hassle. Many opt for an easy store bought sauce, but you'll find quick and simple to make your own from scratch. Follow this recipe, and you'll delicous Alfredo to rival the best in no time!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Kosher salt
  • 1.5 cups whole milk
  • 1.5 cups low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 oz. fetuccini
  • 1.5 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan
  • Fresh chopped parsley to garnish

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Add chicken, season with salt and pepper, then cook 8 minutes per side before resting for 10 minutes
  3. Add milk, broth, and garlic to skillet, then bring to a simmer and add the fettucine
  4. Stir frequently for 3 minutes, then cook 8 minutes more until al dente
  5. Stir in cream and parmesan, simmer till sauce thickens
  6. Remove from heat and stir in sliced chicken, garnishing with parsley
