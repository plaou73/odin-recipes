Baked Mac and Cheese

A favorite amongst all, young and old, surely to make you a hit at the family gathering.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz uncooked macaroni
  • .25 cup butter
  • 2.5 tblspoons all purpose flour
  • 3 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar
  • .5 cup grated parmesan
  • 2 tblspoons butter
  • .5 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cook macaroni according to package instructions. Drain.
  3. Mel butter in a medium skillet over low heat. Gradually add flour, whisking until well combined. Slowly pour milk, whisking till smooth. Stir in cheeses till melted and the sauce is thick
  4. Pour macaroni into large casserole dish, then pour sauce over noodles. Stir well
  5. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat. Add and brown breadcrumbs, then spread over macaroni
  6. Sprinkle with paprike, then bake for 30 minutes. Let rest 5 minutes then serve.
